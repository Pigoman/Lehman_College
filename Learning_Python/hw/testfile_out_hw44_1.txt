New York-New Jersey-Connecticut Tri-State Area 
The New York metropolitan area includes the most populous city in the United States 
(New York City); counties comprising Long Island and the Mid- and Lower Hudson Valley 
in the state of New York; the five largest cities in New Jersey and their vicinities; 
six of the seven largest cities in Connecticut and their vicinities; and five counties in 
Northeast Pennsylvania.
